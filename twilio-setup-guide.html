Troubleshooting & Support

Common Issues:

❌ "SMS not delivered" Error:

  • Check if phone number is verified (required during trial)
  • Verify A2P 10DLC registration is complete
  • Check Twilio logs for specific error messages

❌ "Invalid Phone Number" Error:

  • Ensure phone number is in E.164 format: +1XXXXXXXXXX
  • Include country code (+1 for US)
  • No spaces or special characters except +

❌ Business Verification Rejected:

  • Ensure all documents are clear and readable
  • Match business name exactly across all documents
  • Contact Twilio support for specific rejection reasons
