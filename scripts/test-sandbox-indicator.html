Troubleshooting

If sandbox indicator doesn't appear:

  • Check browser console for errors
  • Verify email is exactly: crushjunkmail@gmail.com or james@familiawashington.com
  • Clear browser cache and localStorage
  • Check that SandboxProvider is wrapping the app
